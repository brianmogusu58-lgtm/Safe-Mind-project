❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How long is each session?

Standard sessions are 50-60 minutes. First sessions may take up to 75 minutes for initial assessment.

What is the cost?

Individual sessions: KES 3,500 - 5,500 depending on therapist. Insurance accepted.

Can I change my appointment?

Yes! Log into your dashboard to reschedule or cancel up to 24 hours before your session.

Is everything confidential?

Absolutely. All sessions are 100% confidential and protected by law.
